Hi, I’m Nico, and I work as an Associate Professor at University of Santiago de Compostela. I’m also affiliated with CiTIUS, the Research Center for Intelligent Technologies. My research focuses on the intersection of Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Medical Imaging.
